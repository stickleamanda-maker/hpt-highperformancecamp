What We Offer

Our program focuses on explosive movement, speed and agility training, and multi-sport games and fitness. We provide elite-level conditioning and technical coaching, including sport-specific skills such as stick handling, shooting, kicking, and passing, for athletes in Hockey, Rugby, Soccer, and Track & Field.

Pricing

Standard Package ($200): Includes a full week of high-intensity training, speed and agility development, comprehensive fitness testing, and personalized skill feedback.

Recovery Package ($250): Includes everything in the Standard Package, plus a daily hydrotherapy session in a heated salt water pool after camp.

Head Instructor

YRDSB Teacher and Coach for over 20+ years, holding a Level One Coaching Certification and coaching with the Aurora Barbarians Rugby Club. With two decades of experience, our lead instructor specializes in building long-term athletic success, elite performance habits, and mental toughness.

Assistant Instructors

Our team includes elite U18 athletes who bring current, high-intensity sport-specific expertise. They bridge the gap between amateur play and elite competition by providing mentorship and real-world athletic experience in Hockey, Rugby, Soccer, and Track.
